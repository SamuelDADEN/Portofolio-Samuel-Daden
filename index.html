Other Projects

Aircraft Design Project: Distributed Thrust Aircraft Design
FAST-OAD (based on OpenMDAO) Excel ALCAZAR (old aircraft design tool from Supaero)

Full aircraft sizing study centred on distributed propulsion (multifan with centralised gas generator). The project covered mass estimation through constraint analysis, wing loading and thrust sizing, weight convergence, aerodynamic and static margin optimisation, and performance analysis. Final performance was poor, as expected, due to the wing design not being adapted for the new propulsion unit.

Aerodynamic Analysis of a Porsche 911 CFD
StarCCM+

Aerodynamic study of the Porsche 911 type 996 using computational fluid dynamics. Work focused on mesh optimisation in StarCCM+ to achieve converged, accurate surface pressure and drag predictions, and analysis of the flow field around the body.
