About

I am an engineering student specialising in aeronautics and mechanical systems. My academic training has led me to explore the intersection of classical mechanics, fluid dynamics, and modern numerical methods — from finite-element analysis to computational aerodynamics.

I am particularly interested in constrained optimisation problems: finding the lightest structure, the most efficient wing shape, or the optimal control-surface sizing — always with physical insight guiding the mathematics.

Feel free to reach out to discuss a project or an opportunity.
